Home Learning Outcome 1 Learning Outcome 2 Learning Outcome 3 Learning Outcome 4 Learning Outcome 5

You create engaging concepts and translate them into interactive validated media products by applying user-centered design principles, visual design techniques and by exploring emerging trends and developments in media, design and technologies.

Explanation
Engaging & validated media products = You find this out through testing: is the target audience attracted, triggered, satisfied or otherwise intrigued to use the concept or product of it.

User-centered design principles = Your digital products are designed and built in accordance with guidelines from the media field, like usability heuristics, persuasive design techniques, UI-patterns, etc…

Emerging technologies = You explore technologies that are relevant for the current age of ICT, like Artificial reality, Mixed reality, Internet of Things, etc…
